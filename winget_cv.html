Main

Jason Winget

Senior Scientist, Proteomics, LC-MS/MS, and Data Science

I am a PhD biochemist with 10 years of experience in proteomics, including quantitative shotgun and targeted approaches. I am a strong advocate for reproducible research, and have focused my personal development on best statistical practices for data analysis and interpretation, as well as clear and transparent communication of results.

I am particularly interested in opportunities which leverage data science (statistical analysis and modeling) to provide deeper insights that address biological questions about mechanism of action and health.
